Question: Who can enter?

Answer: Any team or "crew" of Driver and Navigator can enter any of the GLORY DAYS CLASSIC RALLY events in any type of car. However, we strongly recommend the use of a "classic, historic or vintage" car. Classic cars bring an extra dimension to any rally. However, we realize that enthusiasts who are not familiar with Classic Rallying might wish to give it a try before committing to the purchase of a classic car, or they may just be interested to go for the tour.
